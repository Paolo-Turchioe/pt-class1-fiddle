2016 Summer Vacation

In the summer of 2016, I had the great fortune of going to Italy and Switzerland. My girlfriend and I started the trip in Milan, where my cousin was working at the time. After a few days indulging in excellent food and wine, we continued our trip through the Alps into Switzerland. We arrived in Bern, which was a gracious city bisected by a mineral rich river, the Aarle. The following day we left for Interlaken, a small city situated in the Bernese Highlands. This served as a base camp for many charming day trips to a number of mountains and lakes in the region.
